In a bruised city where power is traded in whispers, one voice refuses to be silenced. Jananayagan follows a reluctant leader drawn into the storm — torn between survival and the price of standing for everyone left behind.

As alliances fracture and the streets ignite, the film traces who truly leads when hope is the last weapon standing — and how far one person will go to shield the people they call home.
